Our Story

About Ember & Veil

A candlelit sanctuary for handcrafted tea, thoughtful tarot, and the kind of quiet that helps you hear yourself again.

Slow Moments, Made Sacred

Ember & Veil was created for people who crave a softer pace: a place to sip, reflect, gather, and reconnect with their own intuition.

Tea as Ritual

Each tea is chosen for its flavor, feeling, and the small pause it creates. We believe tea can turn an ordinary moment into a meaningful one.

Tarot as Reflection

Our readings are conversational, grounded, and compassionate. Tarot is used as a mirror for insight, not a source of fear.

Atmosphere as Care

Candlelight, brass details, warm textures, and intentional quiet all help create an experience that feels restorative and memorable.

Our Philosophy

We do not rush. We do not overwhelm. We create space for curiosity, comfort, conversation, and clarity.
